# SEO Operations (Veck.io Focus)

## Primary keyword
- Main keyword: `Veck.io`
- Core support keywords: `play veck.io online`, `veck.io guides`, `veck.io tips`, `veck.io faq`

## Weekly publishing rhythm
- 3 updates per week minimum:
  - 1 guide update (`/guides/`)
  - 1 FAQ improvement (`/faq/`)
  - 1 news tracking post (`/news/`)

## Measurement checklist
- Connect Google Search Console to `https://veck.app/`
- Connect GA4 and track page-level engagement:
  - Home (`/`)
  - Play (`/play/`)
  - Guides (`/guides/`)
  - FAQ (`/faq/`)
  - News (`/news/`)

## KPI targets
- CTR for queries containing `veck.io`
- Returning users on `/news/` and `/faq/`
- Avg engagement time on `/guides/`
- Top landing pages with high impressions but low CTR (rewrite title/meta)

## Iteration loop
1. Export top queries weekly from Search Console
2. Rewrite weakest title/meta pairs
3. Add one internal link to each updated page
4. Refresh sitemap `<lastmod>` values
