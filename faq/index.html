Veck.io FAQ

Answers for common Veck.io questions from new and returning players.

Why is the embedded Veck.io frame blank?

Refresh the page, then try again. If needed, use fullscreen mode and verify browser extensions are not blocking third-party frames.

What settings should I lock first?

Lock sensitivity and crosshair style for at least one week. Avoid daily setting changes while building mechanics.

How can I improve first-duel win rate?

Pre-aim likely angles, strafe-stop before shooting, and enter with cover options instead of straight-line peeks.

What is a fast daily routine for Veck.io?

15-minute warmup, 3 focused matches, short VOD review of one loss, then one corrective drill.
