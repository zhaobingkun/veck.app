Veck.io Map Route Priority

The fastest way to lose consistency is entering every fight through the same exposed line. Good routing gives you easier shots before your aim even matters.

Last reviewed April 7, 2026Notes from the veck.app editor

Route 1: safest first look

Your first route should show only one likely enemy angle. The goal is not speed. The goal is seeing the duel before the duel sees you.

Route 2: cover-backed re-entry

After first contact, move to a second angle with nearby cover. This stops you from repeating the same peek and becoming predictable.

Route 3: exit line

Always know the route you will use if the duel goes wrong. Many avoidable deaths come from swinging with no fallback plan.

Checklist before pushing

  • Can I isolate one angle first?
  • Do I have cover within one movement beat?
  • Can I leave without crossing open ground?
