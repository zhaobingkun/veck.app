Veck.io Loadout Control by Range

A stable loadout is one that fits the fights you actually take. Start with engagement distance, not theory.

Last reviewed April 7, 2026Notes from the veck.app editor

Close range

Prioritize quick handling and forgiving recovery. You will win more close fights with a consistent first burst than with a build that only feels good on paper.

Mid range

This is where most players should optimize first. Choose for recoil comfort, reliable first shots, and movement that still feels natural while peeking.

Long range

Do not overbuild for edge-case long duels if most of your matches are decided in medium spaces. Precision matters, but only if the rest of the setup stays manageable.

One-change rule

Make one change per session. If you tweak recoil feel, do not also change sensitivity and route timing on the same day.
