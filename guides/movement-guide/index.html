Veck.io Movement Guide

Movement is not just about being hard to hit. It is about arriving at the duel in a state where you can actually win it.

Last reviewed April 7, 2026Notes from the veck.app editor

Strafe-stop before shots

Many players move well until the bullet matters. The important habit is not raw speed. It is stopping cleanly enough to let the shot be intentional.

Enter with cover in mind

Good movement creates future options. If your route gives you no cover and no exit, the movement was bad even if it looked fast.

Use crouch with purpose

Crouch is not a panic button. Use it when it changes the duel in your favor, not as a random habit.
