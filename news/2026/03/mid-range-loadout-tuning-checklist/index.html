Mid-range Loadout Tuning Checklist

Mid-range fights expose shaky setups faster than almost any other situation. The current checklist stays focused on three things: recoil comfort, first-shot reliability, and movement cost.

Short note from the veck.app editor

Recoil comfort

If the weapon only feels usable when you slow down too much, it is not stable enough for a long session.

First-shot reliability

Your first bullet should feel predictable from a stop. If the opening shot keeps drifting off your intended line, adjust before touching anything else.

Movement penalty tolerance

Some players build too hard for static control and lose fights while moving into cover. The better checklist keeps movement in the decision.
