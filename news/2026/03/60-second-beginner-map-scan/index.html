60-second Beginner Map Scan

A quick map scan helps beginners stop entering fights blind. The full routine takes about a minute and resets your focus before the match starts.

Short note from the veck.app editor

What to scan

  • Main entry lanes
  • Reliable cover anchors
  • Nearest fallback route
  • Where the objective flow is likely to pull players

Why it works

Beginners often rush into aim practice while forgetting the map. This scan fixes that by creating simple route expectations before the first duel.

Best use

Run the map scan after warmup and before your first queue if you tend to lose early due to bad positioning.
