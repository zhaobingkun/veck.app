Entry-path Positioning Mistakes to Avoid

The biggest repeated mistake is not poor aim. It is taking the same line into the same danger again and again.

Short note from the veck.app editor

Predictable entry lines

If you always open the same route, opponents get easier reads and your duel quality drops before shots are even fired.

No visible escape plan

Many first-contact losses happen because players push without identifying the nearest cover or fallback line.

Fix

Rotate your first approach more often and keep one escape option available before exposing yourself.
