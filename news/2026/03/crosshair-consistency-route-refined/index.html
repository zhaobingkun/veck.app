Crosshair Consistency Route Refined

The recommendation now starts with smoother tracking before controlled flick work. The change is small, but it reduces the tendency to enter the first match with overactive hands.

Short note from the veck.app editor

What changed

The old order pushed flicks too early. The refined route starts with tracking lanes, then moves into controlled angle jumps. This keeps the first acceleration smoother.

Why it matters

Players who overflick in their opening duels often do not need more speed. They need calmer setup work before speed. The new route addresses that directly.

What to do next

Use the full 3-phase warmup and keep the tracking block strict for the first five minutes.
