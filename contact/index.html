Contact veck.app

Use this page for content corrections, broken Veck.io wrapper reports, outdated settings notes, or suggestions for a missing guide or FAQ topic.

  • Email: hello@veck.app
  • Best for: broken pages, factual fixes, missing topics, and outdated screenshots or settings notes.
  • Include the page URL, what went wrong, and your browser or device when reporting wrapper issues.

Response target: within 48 hours for content fixes and broken-link reports.

What to Include

  • The exact page URL where the problem appears.
  • The issue type: broken link, blank frame, outdated note, wrong setting, or missing topic.
  • Your browser, device type, and whether the issue happens on both the play page and the homepage embed.
  • A screenshot or short description of what you expected to happen.

Email is the primary support channel for veck.app. Public social support accounts are not currently maintained.
